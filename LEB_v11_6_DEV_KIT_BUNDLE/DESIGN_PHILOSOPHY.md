# DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

LEB treats engines as structured systems rather than raw prompts.

## Principles

Governance First\
Every engine must preserve safety boundaries and identity constraints.

Composable Architecture\
Subsystems are layered and optional.

Simulation Before Change\
Major architectural changes should be tested in simulation.

Agent DNA\
Domain-specific expertise is modeled as molecular personality profiles.

Progressive Complexity\
New users see simple commands; advanced users can access the full
architecture.
